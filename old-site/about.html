Our Journey

Founded on the principles of excellence, Vows & Vedas (formerly Exotic Indian Weddings) is represented by a team of creative, enthusiastic, trained, and experienced individuals dedicated to planning and executing your perfect wedding.

We strive to make each wedding a talk of the town. By taking care of every detail, we ensure you can relax and simply look your best. We plan weddings on a range of themes—from the serene beaches and majestic forts to the snowy slopes of the Himalayas and the gushing banks of the Ganges.

Every itinerary is carefully planned to provide the level of activity that suits you best. We leverage our industry connections to provide illustrative arrangements and avoid costly mistakes, helping you live the most beautiful moments of your life with us.
